About Us

A sisterhood built for becoming.

Daughters of Destiny began with a simple conviction: every girl and woman deserves to be seen, equipped, and surrounded by people who believe in her future.

Ecclesiastes 4:12

"A person standing alone can be attacked and defeated, but two can stand back-to-back and conquer. Three are even better, for a triple-braided cord is not easily broken."

What We Do

Purpose of Daughters of Destiny

We create intentional spaces that help young girls and women grow through life's challenges with courage, confidence, and practical support.

01

Navigate Challenges

To help young girls and women to navigate through life's challenges.

02

Blunt Conversations

To have blunt conversations on various conversations affecting women and girls from health, mental and physical wellness, family, relationships, corporate balance, wealth and finances.

03

Achieve More

To assist young women and girls to achieve more and be better in all areas of their lives. Be the best version of themselves.

04

Build Confidence

To help all women and girls in building confidence in themselves.

Why It Matters

When a woman rises, her whole community feels the change.

Empowerment is more than inspiration. It is confidence, knowledge, belonging, and the practical support needed to make brave decisions.

By investing in girls and women, we help families become stronger, leadership become more compassionate, and communities become more hopeful.
